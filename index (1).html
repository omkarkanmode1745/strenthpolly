Who We Are

Strength Polymer Industries

Located in Chimbali, Chakan (Pune), we are a trusted manufacturer of high-quality rubber components used across diverse industrial sectors. We specialize in rubber washers, gaskets, O-rings, bellows, profiles, and rubber-to-metal bonded parts.

Our facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified and equipped with advanced molding machinery to ensure precision and consistency. From material selection to final inspection, every step reflects our commitment to quality and performance.

Driven by innovation and a customer-first approach, we focus on providing customized solutions that meet specific requirements. Whether it's a bulk order or a custom project, we deliver with reliability and professionalism.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name in the global rubber manufacturing industry by delivering reliable, high-performance, and customized rubber solutions — setting new benchmarks in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To manufacture premium rubber components that meet the diverse needs of industries through precision engineering and strict quality control — guided by our ISO 9001:2015 certification and focused on building long-term client relationships.

From the Desk of the Founder

We began with a simple vision — to deliver top-quality rubber components with a focus on trust, innovation, and consistency. Over the years, our journey has been shaped by challenges, but our commitment to excellence has never wavered. Every product reflects our values of precision, durability, and reliability. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification reinforces the high standards we maintain daily. As we grow, our promise remains strong — to keep innovating, adapting, and delivering solutions that add value. Thank you for trusting Strength Polymer Industries.
